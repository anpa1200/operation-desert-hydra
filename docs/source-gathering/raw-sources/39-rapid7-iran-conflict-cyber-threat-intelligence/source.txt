## How to protect your organization

![Early-stage detection matters
most](/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rapid7.com%2Fcdn%2Fimages%2Fbltcc829a5e0010a69c%2F69aed6d14e5c7e00088ac967%2Fcard-
digital-risk-protection.jpg&w=3840&q=75)

### Early-stage detection matters most

Campaigns are starting with initial access attempts, such as suspicious login
activity, password spraying, or exploitation of exposed services. Detect early
and prevent escalation.

![Have full attack surface
visibility](/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rapid7.com%2Fcdn%2Fimages%2Fblta93bc2aa099ff38d%2F69aed6e52b01730008d5f021%2Fcard-
attack-surface-management.jpg&w=3840&q=75)

### Have full attack surface visibility

Correlate telemetry from endpoints, network traffic, identity systems, and
cloud infrastructure fully to understand attacker behavior rather than relying
on isolated alerts.

![Reduce dwell time
](/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rapid7.com%2Fcdn%2Fimages%2Fblt00e0fdbce5229a8e%2F6970b39bb7d8668f895a7f05%2FMDR-
Microsoft-hero-team.jpg&w=3840&q=75)

### Reduce dwell time

Attackers are moving quickly from access to impact. Use automation and well-
defined workflows to remove the speed advantage by creating faster detection,
validation, and response.

![Understand expected attacker
tactics](/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rapid7.com%2Fcdn%2Fimages%2Fblt4180d9ccc3a54fb5%2F69aed6f8cb63a70008936e97%2Fcard-
red-blue-purple-teaming.jpg&w=3840&q=75)

### Understand expected attacker tactics

Iran-linked actors and affiliates rely on well-established techniques, not
novel exploits. Phishing, credential access, DDoS, and edge-device compromise
are high in their playbooks.
