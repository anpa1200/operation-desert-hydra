#  Agrius

[Agrius](/groups/G1030) is an Iranian threat actor active since 2020 notable
for a series of ransomware and wiper operations in the Middle East, with an
emphasis on Israeli
targets.[[1]](https://assets.sentinelone.com/sentinellabs/evol-
agrius)[[2]](https://research.checkpoint.com/2023/agrius-deploys-moneybird-in-
targeted-attacks-against-israeli-organizations/) Public reporting has linked
[Agrius](/groups/G1030) to Iran's Ministry of Intelligence and Security
(MOIS).[[3]](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/business/security-
insider/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/Iran-turning-to-cyber-enabled-influence-
operations-for-greater-effect-05022023.pdf)

ID: G1030

ⓘ
